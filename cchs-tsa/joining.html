How to Join TSA

Joining TSA is a painless process. The only thing you have to do is pay $25 in dues to the TSA chapter advisor (currently Ms. Gant in room 2132). After that, it is recommended that you joining the Remind group for alerts about events and meetings. To join the Remind text @6chedd to 81010.
