Chamblee VEX Robotics

"The Chamblee Vex Robotics Team exists to encourage and inspire students at Chamblee Charter High Schools to pursue experiences and opportunities in STEM through robotics that will instill skills in teamwork, research, design, collaboration, problem solving, critical thinking and building in project based learning. The VEX Robotics Competition, presented by the Robotics Education & Competition Foundation, is the largest and fastest growing middle school and high school robotics program globally with more than 20,000 teams from 50 countries playing in over 1,700 competitions worldwide. Each year, an exciting engineering challenge is presented in the form of a game. Students, with guidance from their teachers and mentors, build innovative robots and compete year-round."
-Mrs. Gant CCHS TSA Chapter Advisor




The Vex Robotics Website can be accessed at https://www.vexrobotics.com
