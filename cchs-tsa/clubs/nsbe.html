NSBE

NSBE (the National Society of Black Engineers) is a student led engineer society focused on the recruitment of black and other minority engineers. To date, it has over 30,000 members worldwide.
Chamblee NSBE Jr. is a vital and vibrant club that exist to expose, encourage and inspire students to pursue STEM experiences and opportunities that will prepare them to be leaders and global citizens that fulfill the mission of NSBE which is to increase the number of culturally responsible Black engineers that excel academically, succeed professionally and positively impact the community.

NSBE's Mission Statement is:
"To increase the number of culturally responsible Black engineers who excel academically, succeed professionally, and positively impact the community."
-NSBE

NSBE's Vision Statement is:
"We envision a world in which engineering is a mainstream word in homes and communities of color, and all Black students can envision themselves as engineers. In this world, Blacks exceed parity in entering engineering fields, earning degrees, and succeeding professionally."
-NSBE



NSBE's Website can be found at https://NSBE.org
